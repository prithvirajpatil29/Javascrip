OPERATORS

Javascript operators are symbols that are used to perform operations on operands

        1. Arthimetic operators => Addition, subtraction, multiplicaition, Division / , modulus %, increment++, decrement --

        2. Comparision(Relational) operator => is equa to ==, identical ===, not equal to !=, not identical !==, greater than >, greater equal to >=, less than or equal to <=
        
        3. Bitwise operator => Bitwise AND & Bitwose OR, Bitwise XOR ^, Bitwise NOT ~ , Bitwise leftshift << , Rightshift >>, Bitwise right shift with zero >>>

        4. Logical Operator => Logical AND &&, or ||, not !

        5. Assignment operator => Assign =, Add and Assign +=, Subtract assign -=, Multi and assign *=, Div and assign /=, Modulus and assign %=
        
        6. Special or Terinary operators => condition ? "true":"flase";
