What is Promise

Promise is built in constructor of javascript

A promise object represents an operation which has produced or will eventaulyy produce a value. Promieses provide a robust way to warap the result of Asynchronous requeest

States of Promise

1.Pending

The underlying operation has not yet completed or neither rejected

2. Fulfilled

The operation has finished and promises is Fulfilled with a response(.then()) is used handke Fulfilled response

3. Rejected

The error occured during the operation is rejected with a reason. (.catch()) is used to catch the error

4. Settled

A promise a said to settled, (either resolved or rejected). Once the promise is settled, it becomes immutable
